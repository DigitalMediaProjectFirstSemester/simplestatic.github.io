About Me

Hi, I'm Razvan.

This is a personal project. It can be perceived as a profile picture of the site. Being the only image with me available here.

I will take advantage of the available space to make a short, more informal presentation of myself. If you have read so far, congratulations and thank you for your interest. Not many people have the patience and interest to read. My passion for visual arts started in high school and although I studied a college with a Digital Media field of study in Cluj-Napoca, I can say that I am mostly self-taught. My strengths in this field are layout editing, due to my experience as a magazine technical editor and editor-in-chief, but also the manipulation of images and illustrations, because my motivation to work is fueled by the possibility of using creativity. Speaking of creativity, I am a visionary and I am happy when I can inspire and be inspired.

If you want to collaborate, you have my personal email in the "contact" menu, if you just want to explore some of my work, visit my social media websites listed below.

Best wishes!
